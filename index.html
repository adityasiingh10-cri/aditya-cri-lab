Cover Drive kaise khele 🏏

Cover Drive cricket ka ek classic aur elegant shot hai jo off-side me khela jata hai. Isme ball ko ground ke along smoothly drive kiya jata hai.

Steps:

  • Ball ko dhyan se dekho (good length ya full ball)
  • Front foot ko ball ki line me aage rakho
  • Head steady rakho
  • Bat ka face straight rakho
  • Ball ko gently ground ke along play karo

⚠️ Practice net me karo, timing bahut important hoti hai.
